Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are needed for the classes?

+

A laptop or tablet with a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone is required. Chromebooks work great too. We recommend a screen size of at least 11 inches for the best experience.

How are the live classes conducted?

+

Classes are conducted live via our secure platform with video, audio, and screen sharing. Each class is 50 minutes long. All sessions are mandatorily recorded for safety and review.

Can I reschedule or cancel a class?

+

Yes! Students can reschedule classes up to 4 hours before the scheduled time through their dashboard. Cancellations are subject to our flexible cancellation policy.

How do you ensure child safety during classes?

+

Every class is mandatorily recorded. Teachers follow a strict code of conduct. No private off-platform communication is allowed. All instructors are background-verified.

Will my child get a certificate after completing a course?

+

Yes! Certificates are auto-generated upon course completion and can be downloaded from the student dashboard. These are great for college applications and extracurricular portfolios.

What is the refund policy?

+

We offer a flexible refund policy. If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, you can request a full refund. Please refer to our refund & cancellation policy page for full details.
